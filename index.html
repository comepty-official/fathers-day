Father’s Day — A Tribute

Happy Father’s Day Daddy

To the man who taught me that love looks like showing up, every single time.

Some men are fathers. You are something rarer — a foundation. From the very first memory I have of you, you have been the greatest father a child could ask for: steady when the world wasn’t, certain when I was not. You lead not with a raised voice but with a raised standard, showing all of us what it means to walk through life with integrity. You have been my first mentor, the quiet teacher behind every lesson I didn’t know I was learning — patience in your hands as you fixed what was broken, wisdom in your words when I had none of my own. I have watched you face mornings that should have broken a lesser man, and somehow you always found a way — not because it was easy, but because giving up was never in your nature. Whenever I have needed strength, I have borrowed yours. Whenever I have needed clarity, your voice has been the one I hear first. You don’t just love your family — you build us, protect us, and believe in us long before we believe in ourselves. Everyone who has ever known you has walked away a little wiser, a little braver, inspired simply by watching how you carry yourself. Today, and every day, I am proud to be your child.
